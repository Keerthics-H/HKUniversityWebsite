Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Follow the steps below to begin your journey at our college:

  1. Submit your application online.
  2. Provide all necessary documentation, including transcripts and letters of recommendation.
  3. Complete any required entrance exams.
  4. Attend an interview, if required.
  5. Receive your admission decision.

Application Requirements

To apply to our college, you will need to submit the following:

  • Completed application form
  • Official high school transcripts
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Standardized test scores (SAT or ACT)
  • Application fee

Application Deadlines

Be sure to submit your application and all required materials by the following deadlines:

  • Early Decision: November 4
  • Regular Decision: January 1
  • Transfer Students: March 1
  • International Students: January 10
